Wave Superposition Interactive Lab

A-Level Physics: phase difference, superposition and interference

Core Principle

When two waves meet, the resultant displacement at each point is the sum of the displacements caused by each wave.

当两个波相遇时，每一点的合位移等于两个波在该点位移的代数和。

yresultant = y1 + y2

What to observe

Watch how the pulses add point-by-point when they overlap, then continue unchanged after meeting.

观察两个脉冲在重叠时如何逐点相加，以及相遇后如何保持原形继续传播。

Continuous Wave Phase

What to observe

Change the phase difference and compare constructive, partial and destructive interference.

改变相位差，比较相长干涉、部分干涉和相消干涉。

Interference type: Constructive interference (in phase)

只有当两列波振幅相等且相位差恰好为 180° 时，才会出现完全相消。

Wavefronts and Rays

What to observe

Wavefront lines join points in the same phase, while rays show the direction of energy transfer.

波前线连接同相位点，射线显示能量传播方向。

Wavefronts connect points that are in the same phase. Rays show the direction of wave travel and are perpendicular to the wavefronts.

波前连接同相位的点。射线表示波的传播方向，并且与波前垂直。

Exam Language (A-Level style)

  • Superposition occurs when waves meet and the resultant displacement is the vector sum of the individual displacements.
  • 当波相遇时会发生叠加，合位移等于各波位移的矢量和。
  • Constructive interference occurs when waves meet in phase, producing a larger amplitude resultant wave.
  • 同相位相遇会产生相长干涉，合成波振幅更大。
  • Destructive interference occurs when waves meet in antiphase, producing a smaller amplitude resultant wave.
  • 反相位相遇会产生相消干涉，合成波振幅减小。

Complete cancellation only occurs when the two waves have equal amplitude and are exactly 180° out of phase.

只有当两列波振幅相等且相位差恰好为 180° 时，才会完全相消。
